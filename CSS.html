H.O.P.E. (Helping Others Pursue English Society) is a registered student-led non-profit dedicated to providing individuals worldwide with an opportunity to learn English through one-on-one tutoring and engaging conversations with native speakers. Our mission is to make English education accessible and affordable for everyone, regardless of their backgrounds or financial situation. Any money generated from classes or fundraising directly contributes to purchasing school supplies and sanitary items for children in underprivileged areas. Additionally, H.O.P.E. is committed to undertaking projects such as writing and donating books to further aid those in need.
